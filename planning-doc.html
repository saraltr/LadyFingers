LadyFinger Website Planning Document

Project Overview

Company Name: LadyFinger

Description: We are a family-run business selling superior quality and authentic art supplies. Our aim is to convey elegance while being approachable.

Website Goals:

  • Facilitate contact with the company
  • Clearly communicate the company's offerings and display their products
  • Landing page with an "About the Team" section
  • Contact, Shop, and Blog pages
  • Encourage users to call for more information

User Interface Design

Visual Elements:

  • Color Palette: Mainly grey to reflect the brand color
  • Typography: Elegants and artistic fonts.

    • Fonts cosideration:

    Harmond

    Indie Flower

    Magilio

    Arterio

    Garet

  • Imagery: High-quality visuals showcasing art supplies and the team

Page Specifics:

  • Landing Page: About the Team section, CTA for contacting the company, trendy design elements
  • Contact Page: Contact information, contact form, map (if applicable)
  • Shop Page: Product catalog, easy-to-use filtering, secure payment integration
  • Blog Page: List of blog posts, readable article layout, social sharing
